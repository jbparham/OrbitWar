BLOCKADE

45 vs 35 pts

Asymmetric shoot-em-up. APU blockades USA. Unbalanced by design — play twice, swap sides.

TOTAL WAR

100 vs 100 pts

Balanced World War III scenario. Full force composition. Ends at 200 VP lead or cease-fire.
